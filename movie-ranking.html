The Best Movies According to Tayyaba

My top 3 movies of all-time

Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is about a man who stays hopeful and strong in prison despite being innocent, and the best part is when he cleverly escapes after years of planning.

Inception

Inception is about a skilled thief who enters people’s dreams to steal secrets, and the best part is the thrilling, mind-bending scene where dreams within dreams blend together in an epic heist.

Shutter island

Shutter Island is about a U.S. Marshal investigating a missing patient at a mental hospital, and the best part is the shocking twist that changes everything he—and the audience—thought was true.
